Welcome to the 30 Seconds or Less page.

30 Seconds or Less is a game currently in development for Android and PC. The goal is to create an enjoyable game which pits the player against a variety of enemies with a wide arsenal of abilities, but with stages which only take up to 30 seconds.

Current Development Goals

1) Fix UI to scale with users screen
2) Add ranged functionality for Basic Attacks.
3) Properly fix level up menu to reflect choice made in class selection
